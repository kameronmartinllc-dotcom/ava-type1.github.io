Your survival guide for the most challenging month

You're Not Alone

Those first 30 days can feel overwhelming, but you've got this. This guide will walk you through each day with practical steps, downloadable tools, and the knowledge that thousands of families have been exactly where you are right now. Take it one day at a time.

Emergency Information

Call 911 if: Your child is unconscious, having seizures, or you can't wake them up

Call your doctor if: Blood sugar is over 400 mg/dL or under 70 mg/dL and not responding to treatment

Always carry: Glucagon emergency kit, fast-acting sugar, medical ID
