Dear Teacher,

I wanted to share some important information about my child, _________________, who has Type 1 Diabetes (T1D). This is an autoimmune condition where the pancreas stops producing insulin, a hormone needed to process sugar from food.

What You Need to Know:

  • It's not caused by diet or lifestyle - it's an autoimmune condition
  • My child needs insulin to process carbohydrates from food
  • Blood sugar levels need to be checked regularly throughout the day
  • Low blood sugar (hypoglycemia) can be dangerous and needs immediate treatment

What to Watch For:

  • Low blood sugar signs: Shaking, sweating, confusion, irritability, hunger
  • High blood sugar signs: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue
  • If you notice these signs: Please send my child to the school nurse immediately

What My Child May Need:

  • Permission to check blood sugar in class if needed
  • Access to water and bathroom without restrictions
  • Snacks or juice available for low blood sugar
  • Understanding if they seem "off" or not themselves
